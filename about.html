Who We Are

Sekure Foundation is dedicated to creating sustainable social impact through initiatives aimed at feeding orphanages, eradicating poverty through empowerment programs, and assisting underprivileged individuals in achieving social integration and personal growth.

Sekure Foundation has the following tier programs:

Food Aid

We provide nutritious meals to orphanages and vulnerable groups, ensuring no one goes hungry.

Skills Training

We offer practical training programs to equip individuals with skills for economic independence.

Educational Support

We provide educational materials and sponsorships to underprivileged youth for a brighter future.

Social Reintegration

We create safe spaces and mentorship programs to help individuals integrate into society.

About the Founder

Ahmed Muhammed Ibrahim (Sekure) is the visionary behind the SEKURE FOUNDATION. With a deep-rooted passion for social justice and a commitment to improving the lives of underserved communities, Sekure has dedicated his life to empowering those in need.

Ahmed Muhammed Ibrahim (Sekure) holds a Bachelor’s degree in Geography from Bayero University Kano, a Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM), and a Master’s in Public Administration. His academic foundation equipped him with a broad understanding of sustainable development and community empowerment strategies.

With a distinguished career working on projects with the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), Sekure gained extensive experience in addressing global challenges, particularly those affecting marginalized populations such as orphans, widows, and youth.

In 2019, motivated by a vision to create tangible change, Sekure founded SEKURE FOUNDATION. The foundation was established with the mission to provide vital support, education, feeding, and vocational training to orphans, widows, and young people, empowering them to build better futures for themselves and their communities.

Through SEKURE FOUNDATION, Sekure has touched countless lives, providing not only resources but also hope and opportunities for a brighter tomorrow. The foundation has become a beacon of light for marginalized populations across [Country/Region], working tirelessly to advocate for their rights and improve their quality of life.

With an unwavering belief in the power of education and sustainable development, Sekure remains committed to making the world a better place, one life at a time.
